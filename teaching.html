Teaching

Our laboratory is actively involved in teaching a range of courses for graduate and undergraduate students. The courses are designed to provide a solid foundation in nonlinear control theory and practical skills in sensing and detection technologies.

Master Courses

  • Fundamentals of Nonlinear Control Theory (Master)

Undergraduate Courses

  • Introduction to Nonlinear Control (Undergraduate)
  • Sensors and Detection Technologies (Undergraduate)
