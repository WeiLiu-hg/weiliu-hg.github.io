Selected Journal

The following is a selection of our representative publications. These works demonstrate the theoretical foundations of our lab's research efforts. If you are interested in our research, we welcome you to follow our work and contact us for collaborations or inquiries.

  1. W. Liu and J. Huang, Cooperative global robust output regulation for nonlinear output feedback multiagent systems under directed switching networks. IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, full paper, vol. 62, no. 12, pp. 6339-6352, December 2017.
  2. W. Liu and J. Huang, Event-triggered global robust output regulation for a class of nonlinear systems. IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, vol. 62, no. 11, pp. 5923-5930, November 2017.
  3. W. Liu and J. Huang, Cooperative global robust output regulation for a class of nonlinear multi-agent systems with switching network. IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, vol. 60, no. 7, pp. 1963-1968, July 2015.
  4. W. Liu and J. Huang, Adaptive leader-following consensus for a class of higher-order nonlinear multiagent systems with directed switching networks. Automatica, regular paper, vol. 79, pp. 84-92, May 2017.
  5. W. Liu and J. Huang, Cooperative global robust output regulation for a class of nonlinear multi-agent systems by distributed event-triggered control. Automatica, regular paper, vol. 93, pp. 138-148, July 2018.
  6. W. Liu and J. Huang, Event-triggered cooperative robust practical output regulation for a class of linear multi-agent systems. Automatica, vol. 85, pp. 158-164, November 2017.
  7. W. Liu and J. Huang, Cooperative adaptive output regulation for lower triangular nonlinear multiagent systems subject to jointly connected switching networks. IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, vol. 31, no. 5, pp. 1724-1734, May 2020.
  8. W. Liu and J. Huang, Event-triggered cooperative global robust practical output regulation for second-order uncertain nonlinear multiagent systems. IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, regular paper, vol. 29, no. 11, pp. 5486-5498, November 2018.
  9. J. Luo and W. Liu, Leader-following consensus with prescribed performance for linear multi-agent systems. IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, vol. 70, no. 2, pp. 1402-1409, February 2025.
  10. B. Wu and W. Liu, Event-triggered cooperative exponential practical tracking for a class of higher-order uncertain nonlinear multiagent systems. IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems, regular paper, vol. 53, no. 11, pp. 6931-6942, November 2023.
