Biography

The Intelligent Cooperation and Nonlinear Control was established at the School of Automation Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology in 2021. It is directed by Prof. Wei Liu. Its research areas include cooperative control of multi-agent systems, nonlinear control, modeling and control of robots, and unmanned aerial vehicles.
